Overview

Enterprise administration at scale

TalentReef serves large organizations managing thousands of hourly employees across hundreds of locations — each with its own managers, roles, and permission levels.

I led research and concept design for the administration experience: making user management, role assignment, and permissions feel simple at enterprise scale.

User Account Management, the administrative home for enterprise organizations. Click to expand.

The Challenge

Four problems every admin faced

Thousands of employees

Onboarding a location meant setting up 20+ users, one at a time.

Complex permissions

Custom hierarchies didn't map to default permission sets.

Time-consuming admin

Routine account changes took too many steps and too much time.

Information overload

No visibility into who had access to what — or why.

Design Decisions

Why it works

Designing for speed

Admin time is expensive. Every workflow was measured in clicks and redesigned until routine tasks felt instant.

Simplifying permissions

Permissions became visible, named, and chip-based — no more guessing what a role could actually do.

Supporting large datasets

Tables, filters, and pagination were designed for the 20,000-user org first — so smaller orgs simply feel effortless.

Reducing repetitive work

Bulk actions, saved filters, and sensible defaults eliminated the busywork that made admins dread the tool.
