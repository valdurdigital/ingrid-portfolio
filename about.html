[ How I work ]

Clarity is the job.
Everything else follows.

I'm at my best when a problem is still messy. When the team isn't sure what they're building or why. I like getting into those rooms early, asking the obvious question nobody's asked yet, then helping everyone agree on what we're actually solving.

I work closely with engineers and PMs, not handing things off, but figuring things out together. I run workshops, sketch and prototype early with help of AI, help me make things faster. Confidence comes from testing, not from waiting.

I use AI tools every day, my workflow has changed for the past months. They've made me faster at the parts that don't need me, which means I can spend more time on the parts that do.
