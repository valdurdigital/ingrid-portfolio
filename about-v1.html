Most of my work lives in the long, awkward middle of a system — the part where teams disagree about what the product is for.

I started out at a small studio in Halifax doing civic and health-sector work, then spent five years at Shopify and Northbridge before joining Maple & Reed in 2022. Along the way I became the person on a team who likes the unglamorous problems best — the ones that won't fit on a slide.

I write a lot, prototype early, and try not to make things that need a tour to use. I read everything I ship to a non-designer before it ships. Outside of work I'm slowly learning to bind books, and quickly learning that I'm not very good at it yet.
